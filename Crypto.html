Shift-Ciphers

Shift ciphers is one of the simplest and most widely known encryption techniques. It is a type of substitution cipher in which each letter in the plaintext is replaced by a letter some fixed number of positions down the alphabet. Like if we take incrementer 3, then A will become D (A shifted 3 positions ahead.)
